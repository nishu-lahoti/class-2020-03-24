Scene 3

Prompt: Create a bootstrap sample of a 1,000 replications of the mean income of the commuters, where each replication draws the appropriate number, with replacement, from the original data.

set.seed(1)
train_bootstrap <- train %>%
  rep_sample_n(size = 115, replace = TRUE, reps = 1000) %>%
  group_by(replicate) %>%
  summarize(summary_average = mean(income))

ggplot(train_bootstrap, aes(x=summary_average)) + 
  geom_histogram(bins = 20) +
  labs(x = "Average Income",
       y = "Count")
